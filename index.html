Welcome to Home Cinema


Discover new worlds, relax and watch great movies at Home Cinema. Our cinema is open everyday from 10 a.m. to 10 p.m. so you can enjoy your favourite movies anytime you want!

We own great quality audio and video equipment so you can have the best experience possible. Our comfortable chairs will make you feel like you are in the comfort of your own home. We provide special rooms for you and your friends if you come in 10-20 people/group. A smaller, cozy place with couches instead of chairs, blankets and pillows.


Grab a snack


Before the movie starts don't forget your snack and drink! Our snack bar will not disappoint you. You can find popcorn(in different flavours, of course), peanuts, nachos, candy bars, chocolate bars and much more. Besides the classic drinks such as soda and water we also have non-alcoholic cocktails made by our one and only bartender.

Check our promotions and sales to save some money and get great deals. Grab a snack, grab a friend and enjoy your movie!
